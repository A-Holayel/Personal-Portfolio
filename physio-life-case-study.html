Physio Life Clinic

A complete visual identity designed to inspire trust and wellness.

Project Details

  • Client: Physio Life Clinic
  • Role: Brand Identity, Graphic Design
  • Timeline: 4 Weeks
  • Deliverables: Logo, Brand Guidelines, Stationery, Social Media Kit

The Challenge

To create a modern, trustworthy, and professional brand identity for a new physiotherapy clinic. The brand needed to convey expertise and care while maintaining a contemporary aesthetic to stand out in a competitive healthcare market.

Crafting the Identity

Logo Design

The logo cleverly fuses human anatomy into the clinic's initials, 'P' and 'L'. The letter 'P' integrates the spine to symbolize the body's core structure, while the 'L' is ingeniously formed from the lower limb to represent restored mobility. Embedded icons, including a brain and knee joint, further highlight the clinic's specialized and holistic approach, creating an intelligent and memorable brand mark.

Watch the logo animation to see how the design elements come together.

Color & Typography

The color palette balances clinical trust with restorative energy, using a deep teal to establish expertise and a vibrant cyan to symbolize vitality. This is paired with a modern, geometric sans-serif typeface whose bold, clean lines ensure legibility and convey confidence, making the brand feel both authoritative and accessible across all media.
